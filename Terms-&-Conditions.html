Returns & Replacement

Our product is held to the highest standards for quality and freshness. Should there be a concern that the product as received is unsatisfactory, a claim must be made within 24 hours of receipt of the delivery. Credit will not be granted if unsatisfactory items are discarded or partially consumed. We will not issue credit for product that has been discarded or partially consumed.
